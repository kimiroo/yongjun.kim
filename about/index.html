About me

Yongjun Kim
김용준

I'm an ordinary university student living in Seoul, S. Korea who's just interested in computers and technologies. I'm currently majoring in Business Administration, but it has been the computer that made my heart pound. I've built file and web servers in my senior year in high school when I should have been studying for the SAT. I've also created a prank android app for my friend, which was sadly never been triggered due to my friend's lack of curiosity and created few simple tools and games just for fun. Outside the computer world, I enjoy modding, fixing, and creating things. For example, I modded my keyboard to have a swappable USB port, fixed the family car's internal navigation system with some soldering.

However, my skills were my limits. I didn't learn about coding, electronics nor electricity. This lack of knowledge has limited my ability to fulfill my imagination, or even my ability to imagine. Therefore, I need to learn about this stuff. This is why I decided to create this site. (And it seemed very cool to have a domain with my name. lol) Besides, since I am now serving South Korea's mandatory military service (thanks, Kim Jong-un) and I now have free time to spare, I thought it would be a perfect opportunity to learn and share what I'm learning, making, etc.
